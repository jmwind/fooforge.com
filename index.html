Welcome to me!

I'm Mike and measured by my beard I am an operations guy. Measured by my github account, I'm an infrastructure coder and a (so far) hobbyist Ruby enthusiast.

I'm currently working as an ops guy at XING in Hamburg, Germany and as a freelancing author for international Linux magazines published by Linux New Media AG.
I am LPIC-1 and LPIC-2 certified. You may usually talk to me in person at the OSDC, OSMC and Nordic Ruby conferences.

Contributing to Open Source software plays an important role. I'm an approved contributor to Chef. I also have a couple of old, outdated and unmaintained Plugins at Nagios Exchange.

As a Ruby hobbyist I've started development on Hoarder, a bookmark.app, and imgfoo, reinventing photo galleries on your own server.
